Program Description & Schedule

Our 8-week CrossFit program is meticulously designed to build your strength, endurance, and overall fitness from the ground up. Each phase progressively challenges you, ensuring continuous improvement.

Master the basic movements and proper form to build a strong foundation.

Weeks 3-4: Intensity Boost

Increase the intensity and complexity of exercises, pushing your limits.

Weeks 5-6: Complex Workouts

Introduce multi-joint movements and advanced programming to enhance performance.

Weeks 7-8: Competition Preparation

Fine-tune your skills and prepare for challenges with peak conditioning.
